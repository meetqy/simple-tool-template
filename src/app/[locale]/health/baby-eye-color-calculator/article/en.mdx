# Baby Eye Color Calculator

## What is Baby Eye Color Calculator?

The **Baby Eye Color Calculator** is a tool designed to predict the possible eye color of a baby based on the eye colors of the parents. By selecting the eye colors of both parents, the calculator provides the likelihood of the baby having a specific eye color, categorized into Heterozygous (More likely) and Homozygous (Less likely) probabilities.

## How to Use the Baby Eye Color Calculator

1. Select the eye color of Parent 1 from the dropdown menu.
2. Select the eye color of Parent 2 from the dropdown menu.
3. Click the "Calculate" button to see the results.
4. The results will display the probabilities of the baby having different eye colors.
